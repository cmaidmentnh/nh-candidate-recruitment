Queries the FEC OpenFEC API for individual political donors in NH. Itemized contributions over the minimum amount. Results are cross-referenced against the voter file to identify Republicans and Undeclared voters in target districts.

How Scoring Works
SignalScore
Donation to RNC / NRCC / NRSC+12-15
Donation to any Republican committee+10
Donation to conservative PAC+8
Any political donation+3
Donation amount ≥ $1,000+5 bonus
Donation amount ≥ $500+3 bonus

Scores accumulate across multiple donations. A donor who gave $1,000 to the RNC and $500 to a Republican candidate would score 15 + 5 + 10 + 3 = 33.
